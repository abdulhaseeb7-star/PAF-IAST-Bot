Eligibility Criteria
Only students who have completed the semester with the standard course load as outlined in the Scheme of Studies (SOS) are considered part of the eligible cohort for inclusion in the merit scholarship.
Merit scholarships are not awarded based on final semester results.
No. Of Slots
Semester Merit Scholarship policy states that:
One scholarship if the number of students remains below 30.
Two scholarships if the number of students remains between 30 and 60.
The number of scholarships per program shall increase accordingly.
Important Note
As per the policy, academically relevant UG programs will be merged where the number of enrolled students falls below the half of the threshold of class strength, i.e. 30 as required for the disbursement of Talent Award and Semester Merit Scholarship. The scholarship management committee (SMC) will recommend such merging cases for the approval of the competent authority within a faculty.
