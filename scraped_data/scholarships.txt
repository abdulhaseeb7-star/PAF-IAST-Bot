Scholarships
FINANCIAL AID & STUDENTS DEVELOPMENT
The Aim of the financial aid office at PAF-IAST is to provide assistance to the students in meeting their educational costs during their current academic program. In this regard, University offers vast numbers of Scholarships for students in both merit as well as need-based categories.
Internal Scholarships
Merit-based scholarships
are awarded to students who stand out with strong academics (granted on the basis of admissions merit and semester results).
However,
purely need-based scholarships
are awarded to support less privileged students who face financial constraints to pursue their studies.
University believes to enhance the educational capabilities of the students by off-loading their financial burden and reaching out to financially challenging students.
External Scholarships
Students of PAF-IAST can avail variety of external Scholarships like Bait-ul-Mal Need based Financial Assistance, Workers Welfare Board Directorate of Education KPK Scholarship, Frontier Education Foundation Scholarship Program Fauji Foundation Excellence Award.
