Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology
International Outreach Office at
Our Partner Universities
PAFIAST offers its students the opportunity to spend a semester abroad at one of
its numerous partner universities, to acquire joint or double degrees
and/or carry out an internship in an international organization.
Welcome to the International Office at Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST)! Join us at our beautiful and green campus alongside next generation leaders and thinkers in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship – from around the globe. As you excel on this academic journey, the International Office is here to provide you with essential information and support to life at PAF-IAST.
