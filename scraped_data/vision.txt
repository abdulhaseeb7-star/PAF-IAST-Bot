Vision
Vision
To be an academic leader in foreseeing and addressing the techno-industrial demands and socio-economic challenges of society.
Mission
To produce graduates and entrepreneurs having skills and competence to effectively lead and efficiently manage the techno-industrial and socioeconomic domains of the society relevant to the fields of their choice, by providing them with easy access to world-class techno-industrial facilities and
business ventures and help them groomed in an intellectually stimulating environment of exceptional academic standards conducive for the generation of innovative ideas and supportive of entrepreneurship.
